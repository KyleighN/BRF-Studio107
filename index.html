Chealsey Sanchez

Cosmetologist

Hello Beautiful, my name is Chealsey Sanchez and I’ve been a cosmetologist for 11 years. I received my education at The Salon Professional Academy in La Crosse Wisconsin. I was born and raised in Black River Falls but it wasn’t until after my husband and I were married we chose to make Black River our home. We currently live here with our amazing daughter and fur babies. I love being a cosmetologist and look forward to coming to work. My favorite things about working in the industry are making people feel pampered, beautiful, and the connections I get to have with my guests. Working in a small town allows me to build beautiful relationships, fills my heart, and fuels my passion for cosmetology.
